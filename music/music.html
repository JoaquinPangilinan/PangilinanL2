My Beats

A collection of my favorite tunes

Jazz

I'll Take Care of You

Artist: Richard Poon

Album: I'll take care of you

Release Year: 2008

Richard Poon's "I'll Take Care of You," released in 2008 on his debut album of the same name, is a romantic ballad that blends jazz and pop influences, solidifying his "big band crooner" style. The song earned him the Awit Award for Best Performance by a New Male Recording Artist and a nomination for Best Male Performance. The album, produced by Cornerstone Talent Management Center and distributed by MCA Music, featured covers of popular songs. The album was later released as a platinum edition.

R & B

Kung Sakali

Artist: Michael Pangilinan

Album: Michael Pangilinan

Release Year: 2014

Michael Pangilinan's "Kung Sakali" is a deeply emotional Filipino ballad that explores themes of unconditional love and potential heartbreak. The song's popularity stems from its heartfelt lyrics and Pangilinan's powerful vocal delivery, making it a staple in Filipino music. Released under ABS-CBN Star Music, it has garnered millions of views online, solidifying its place as a beloved ballad. The song resonates with listeners due to its raw expression of enduring love and acceptance, even in the face of pain.

Ballad

Narito

Artist: Gary Valenciano

Album: Faces of Love

Release Year: 1990

Gary Valenciano's "Narito" is a deeply impactful Filipino pop ballad, renowned for its inspirational and spiritual themes. Written by Valenciano himself, the song delivers a powerful message of faith, hope, and the comforting presence of God, offering solace during challenging times. As one of his signature pieces, "Narito" showcases his exceptional vocal talent and emotional depth, cementing its place as a beloved and culturally significant song in the Philippines, frequently used in religious and reflective settings.
